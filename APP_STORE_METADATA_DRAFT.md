## Promotional Text

## Full Description
Habit Tracker helps you build consistency with a simple daily system.

Create habits, set a start date, choose recurrence rules, and mark planned or completed progress by date. Review your streaks and weekly progress to stay motivated.

Premium unlocks unlimited habits and advanced tracking support while the free version is perfect for getting started.

### Key Features
- Create, edit, and organize habits
- Plan and complete habits by date
- Flexible recurrence rules
- Current and best streak tracking
- Weekly and daily progress summaries
- Reminder notifications
- Widget support for quick progress at a glance

## Keywords

The free version supports up to 3 habits.
Premium is a one-time unlock that enables unlimited habits and future premium updates.
